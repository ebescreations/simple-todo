Privacy Policy

Simple To-Do Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Simple To-Do is a browser extension that helps you manage a personal task list. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how.

Data we collect

None. Simple To-Do does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The extension has no analytics, no tracking, and no network requests of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Your tasks are saved in your browser's localStorage — stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your browser
  • Is never sent to any server
  • Is never shared with any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by removing the extension or clearing your browser's local storage

Permissions

Simple To-Do requests no special browser permissions. It does not access your browsing history, tabs, cookies, or any other browser data.

Third parties

Simple To-Do does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

Children's privacy

Simple To-Do does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
